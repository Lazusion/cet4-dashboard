✍️ 作文模板

模板一 · 观点对比型（最常考！）
[第一段]
Recently, the issue of ____ has aroused wide concern.
Some people hold the view that ____, while others argue that ____.
Personally, I tend to support the former/latter.

[第二段]
There are several reasons accounting for my choice.
To begin with, ____. For example, ____.
Furthermore, ____. It is widely acknowledged that ____.

[第三段]
Admittedly, ____. However, this does not mean that ____.
In conclusion, I firmly believe that ____.
Only in this way can we ____.
模板二 · 现象分析型
[第一段]
In recent years, ____ has become increasingly prevalent.
This phenomenon has drawn public attention.

[第二段]
Several factors contribute to this trend.
First of all, with the rapid development of ____, ____.
Moreover, ____ also plays a significant role.

[第三段]
From my perspective, ____ is a double-edged sword.
Therefore, it is high time that we took effective measures.
模板三 · 应用文/书信
Dear ____,

I am writing this letter to ____.

____ (具体内容，3-4句)

Thank you for your time and consideration.
I am looking forward to your early reply.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
刘晓燕极简版（词汇量不够用这个）
[第一段 2句]
Nowadays, ____ has become a hot topic.
Different people have different opinions on this issue.

[第二段 3-4句]
Some people think that ____. They believe that ____.
However, others hold the opposite view.
As for me, I agree with the former/latter.

[第三段 2句]
In conclusion, ____.
Only in this way can we ____.
🎯 万能金句
开头：With the rapid development of society... / Nowadays, there is a growing awareness that...
中间：It goes without saying that... / There is no doubt that... / Every coin has two sides.
结尾：Only by doing so can we... / It is high time that we took effective measures.
🔤 王江涛词汇升级表
but → however / nevertheless
so → therefore / consequently
and → moreover / furthermore
I think → from my perspective
many → numerous / a great number of
important → significant / essential
good → beneficial / favorable
bad → harmful / detrimental
very → extremely / remarkably

🌐 翻译

1 是…的 → It is... that...
2 越来越… → more and more / increasingly
3 不仅…而且… → not only... but also...
4 据说/据报道 → It is said/reported that...
5 被用来… → ... is used to...
6 作为… → As..., ...
7 …的特点是… → ... is characterized by...
8 …起源于… → ... originated from / dates back to
9 被看作是… → ... is regarded/viewed as...
10 扮演重要角色 → plays an important role in...
🆘 不会的词怎么办？3招
1. 上位词代替：包子→food，沙发→furniture
2. 解释性表达：针灸→a medical treatment using needles
3. 大胆跳过：不影响主干就跳过去！

🔬 名师验证技巧

🎧 听力：新闻前3句 + 预读清单
新闻前3句原则：90%答案在前3句！

播音前30秒预读：①横读扫话题 ②竖读找差异 ③圈人名/数字/否定 ④预判问题

陷阱词：but/however→后面是重点 · actually→这才是真话 · I'm afraid→委婉拒绝
📖 阅读：题文同序 + 2+1 + 词性排除
仔细阅读：题目顺序 = 文章段落顺序
匹配题2+1：一段最多匹配2题
选词填空：①标15词词性 ②看空格语法 ③缩到2-3词再选
🆘 蒙题统计规律
① 仔细阅读：同义改写=答案，照抄原文=干扰项
② 听力ABCD均匀分布（约6-7题每个）
③ 选词填空全蒙同一词性至少对2-3题
④ 选项有must/never/all/none往往是错的
