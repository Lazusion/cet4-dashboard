五、作文逻辑连接词(全)

来源:新东方(04.25/04.27/02.22) · 简书(06.03) · 知乎 · 百度文库

① 递进/补充 (moreover类)

furthermore · moreover · in addition · besides · additionally · what's more · likewise · similarly · not only…but also… · as well as

② 转折/让步 (however类)

however · nevertheless · nonetheless · whereas · while · yet · on the contrary · in contrast · conversely · although / though · even though · despite / in spite of · admittedly

③ 因果 (therefore类)

therefore · thus · hence · consequently · as a result · accordingly · due to · owing to · because of · for this reason · the reason is that · this is because

④ 举例 (for example类)

for example · for instance · such as · namely · a case in point · take…as an example · to illustrate · in particular · specifically · particularly

⑤ 总结 (in conclusion类)

in conclusion · to sum up · in summary · in a word · in brief · in short · all in all · on the whole · to conclude · overall · generally speaking

⑥ 时间/顺序

first(ly) · second(ly) · third(ly) · to begin with · first of all · next · then · afterward · meanwhile · subsequently · eventually · finally · last but not least

⑦ 观点/强调

in my opinion · from my perspective · as far as I'm concerned · in fact · indeed · actually · obviously · clearly · undoubtedly · there is no doubt that · it is worth noting that

⑧ 条件/目的

if · unless · as long as · provided that · in order to · so that · so as to · for the purpose of

⑨ 重申/解释

in other words · that is to say · namely · essentially · to put it another way

⚠️ 位置规则（重要！放错会扣分）

只能放句首(后跟逗号):
However, / Therefore, / Moreover, / Furthermore,
In conclusion, / For example, / To sum up, / Nevertheless,
In my opinion, / From my perspective,

只能放句中(连接句子,不加逗号):
because / since / so that / in order to / although / unless
while / whereas / as / when / if

句首或句中都可以:
but / and / so / or

考场口诀: 不确定就往句首放，后跟逗号，永远正确！

七、考前终极提醒

来源:江苏省教育考试院 · 新东方(06.05) · 西安财经大学行知学院

⚠️ 作文重要提醒

2026年起严禁套用模板! 阅卷系统能识别模板化开头,
如果开头和3000人雷同将直接划为低分档。
应对策略: ①自己组合2-3个句型的顺序
②模板句中间插入1个具体关键词
③用简单句+逻辑词串联,比硬套模板更安全
推荐写法: 全简单句 + 每2-3句用一个逻辑词串联

考场时间

09:00-09:28 写作(28min,写完不能改!)
09:30-09:55 听力(25min,边听边涂卡!)
09:55-10:15 仔细阅读C(每题14.2分)
10:15-10:27 长篇匹配B
10:27-10:55 翻译
10:55-11:05 选词填空A(没时间就蒙)
不作弊!答错不扣分,空题零分,全部填满!

应急技巧

手抖/紧张: 开考先写作文首句模板稳定情绪 → 握拳放松交替5次 → 深呼吸(吸4秒呼6秒)
听力漏听: 立刻跳过,绝不停留!纠结一题=丢后面3题
阅读卡壳: 默念题干关键词3次 → 下一题 → 最后回头
翻译不会的词: ①上位词(包子→food) ②解释表达 ③拼音!
选词填空: 标15词词性(n/v/adj/adv) → 看空格语法 → 缩到2-3词再选 → 全蒙同一词性至少对3题
证件丢失: 立即找考务办,凭电子准考证+学籍信息申请临时证明
条形码破损: 举手申请更换答题卡,切勿自行处理
