2026年6月三大预测方向 新东方/有道考神

来源: 新东方2026.06.04六大高频主题 + 有道考神2026.06.04十大话题
方向一：人工智能与学习（最高概率）
  命题: AI辅助学习是否削弱独立思考？
  核心词: artificial intelligence, digital literacy, independent thinking,
          over-reliance on AI, human-machine collaboration, harness
  万能句: AI is a powerful tool that helps us learn better,
          but we should not rely on it completely.

方向二：文化传承与创新
  命题: 如何保护和传承传统文化？
  核心词: cultural heritage, intangible cultural heritage,
          cultural confidence, national identity, inherit, preserve,
          revitalize, traditional craftsmanship
  万能句: Only by combining tradition with innovation can we
          keep our culture alive.

方向三：绿色环保与低碳生活
  命题: 个人如何为绿色发展做贡献？
  核心词: sustainable development, carbon neutrality, biodiversity,
          low-carbon lifestyle, green travel, environmental awareness
  万能句: Small actions make a big difference. Every individual
          can contribute to a greener planet.

完整9类衔接词速查 简书·奇速教育2026.06.03

一、开头

to begin with首先first of all第一generally speaking总体来讲

二、结尾

in conclusion / to sum up总之in a word / in brief简言之

三、先后顺序

first, second, thirdnextmeanwhileeventuallyfinally

四、因果

therefore / thus / consequently因此due to / because of由于
as a result of由于…的结果accordingly于是

五、对比

however / whereas然而on the contrary相反
on the other hand另一方面in contrast with与…对照

六、举例

for example / for instance例如namely即a case in point恰当例子

七、强调（加分！）

furthermore / moreover / besides此外/而且in fact / indeed实际上
in particular尤其not only…but also…不仅…而且

八、综合逻辑

递进: furthermore, moreover, in addition, besides, additionally
转折: however, nevertheless, whereas, while, on the contrary
因果: therefore, consequently, thus, as a result, due to
条件: if so, if possible, unless, as long as
归纳: in short, in brief, in summary

九、陈述观点

in my opinion / I think      在我看来
from my perspective           从我的角度看
as far as I'm concerned       就我而言
as far as I know              据我所知
actually / as a matter of fact 事实上

四、翻译应急技巧

不会的词 → 用上位词代替（包子→food）
不会的词 → 用解释（针灸→treatment using needles）
不会的词 → 用拼音也行！（饺子→jiaozi）
太长的句子 → 切成2-3个短句
时态不确定 → 全用一般现在时
