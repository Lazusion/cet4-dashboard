## 🎯 2026年6月 三大预测方向

### 方向一：人工智能与学习（最高概率）
- 命题：AI 辅助学习是否削弱独立思考？
- 核心词：artificial intelligence, digital literacy, independent thinking, over-reliance on AI, human-machine collaboration
- 万能句：AI is a powerful tool that helps us learn better, but we should not rely on it completely.

### 方向二：文化传承与创新
- 命题：如何保护和传承传统文化？
- 核心词：cultural heritage, intangible cultural heritage, cultural confidence, national identity, inherit, preserve
- 万能句：Only by combining tradition with innovation can we keep our culture alive.

### 方向三：绿色环保与低碳生活
- 命题：个人如何为绿色发展做贡献？
- 核心词：sustainable development, carbon neutrality, low-carbon lifestyle, green travel, environmental awareness
- 万能句：Small actions make a big difference. Every individual can contribute to a greener planet.

## 📋 完整 9 类衔接词

> 来源：简书·奇速教育 (2026.06.03)

### 一、开头
to begin with / first of all / in the first place / generally speaking

### 二、结尾
in conclusion / to sum up / in a word / in brief / in short

### 三、先后顺序
first, second, third / next / meanwhile / eventually / finally

### 四、因果
therefore, thus, consequently / due to, because of / as a result of / accordingly

### 五、对比
however, whereas / on the contrary / on the other hand / in contrast with / similarly

### 六、举例
for example, for instance / namely / a case in point

### 七、强调（加分！）
furthermore, moreover, besides / in fact, indeed / in particular / not only…but also…

### 八、综合逻辑
- 因果：consequently, as a result of this
- 转折：even though, though
- 并列递进：not only…but also…, as well as
- 对比：on the contrary, conversely
- 条件：if so, if possible
- 归纳：in short, in brief

### 九、陈述观点
in my opinion / I think / from my perspective / as far as I'm concerned / as far as I know / actually / as a matter of fact
